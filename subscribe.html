Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a Trend Forge subscription?

Plans include access to articles, newsletters, premium content, ad-free browsing, archives, digital print editions, webinars, and priority support, depending on the tier.

How do I pay with Pi Network (PI)?

Subscriptions are payable via Pi Network wallets. After selecting a plan, you’ll be prompted to send the specified PI amount to our wallet address. Note: PI payments are subject to network availability and mainnet stability.

What is the USD equivalent of PI prices?

Based on recent estimates (~$0.60/PI), Digital is ~$3.60/month, Premium is ~$5.40/month, All-Access is ~$7.20/month, and Lifetime is ~$59.40 one-time. Prices are fixed in PI, so USD equivalents may vary with PI’s market value.[](https://www.coinbase.com/price/pi-network)[](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/pi/)

Can I cancel my subscription?

Monthly plans can be canceled anytime via your account. Lifetime Access is a one-time payment with no recurring fees. Contact support for assistance.

Is the Lifetime Access plan transferable?

Lifetime Access is tied to your account and non-transferable. It provides perpetual access to all Trend Forge content and benefits.
