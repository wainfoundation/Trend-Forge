Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trend Forge?

Trend Forge is a news platform offering in-depth analysis on World, Business, Finance, Science, and Culture. Learn more on our About page.

How do subscriptions work?

We offer Digital (6 PI/month), Premium, All-Access, and Lifetime plans, payable via Pi Network cryptocurrency. Visit Subscribe to choose a plan.

What is Pi Network?

Pi Network is a mobile-first cryptocurrency used for our subscriptions. Learn more at Pi Network News.

How do I access premium content?

Premium articles (e.g., "Tensions Rise in Asia-Pacific Trade Talks") require a subscription. Non-subscribers see a prompt to subscribe.

How do I contact support?

Use our Contact form or email support@trendforge.com.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use HTTPS, encrypted storage, and GDPR-compliant practices. See our Security page.
