Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

At Trend Forge, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or subscribe to our services.

We use your information to:

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with payment processing (e.g., Pi Network), analytics, or customer support.

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Please safeguard your account credentials.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us at support@trendforge.com.
  • Opt out of newsletters or marketing emails via the unsubscribe link.

6. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@trendforge.com or via our Contact page.
